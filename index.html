Xiaoyu Fan

I am a forth-year PhD student in Operations Management at NYU Stern School of Business, where I am advised by Professor Zhengyuan Zhou. Before joining NYU Stern, I obtained my Bachelor's degree in Information and Computational Science from Nanjing University Mathematics Department.

I am broadly interested in data-driven decision making, with a special focus on designing sample-efficient policy learning algorithms for problems in operations management. I develop and leverage tools from machine learning, stochastic processes and game theory. Some of my research projects focus on understanding the learning limit of the stochastic systems that arise in inventory control, revenue management and reinforcement learning.
